About Elora...

Elora is a certified birth and postpartum doula in Nampa, Idaho. She specialized in holistic care for mothers during this transformative period.

Through deep connection with my clients, I hope to help them achieve the birth and/or postpartum they desire. I believe that although much of mothering is a learned skill, you hold within you the innate knowledge, power, and intuition to birth and care for your baby.

When I'm not working with clients, you can find me with my own two babies, usually at home.

Nurturing Care During Pregnancy, Birth, and Postpartum

Birth can be an empowering experience. Through education, physical, and emotional support, a birth doula can assist you in having the birth you dream of.

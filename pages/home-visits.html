Home Visits

I am so honored to be welcomed into my clients' homes during such a special time.

During home visits, my goal is to empower families to trust their intuition so that they can be the amazing parents they are meant to be.

Hourly Rate: $40 - $50 Sliding Scale

During home visits, I offer:

  • Nourishing meals brought to you in bed
  • Emotional/Birth processing
  • Helping build your village
  • An extra set of hands to hold baby if you need to shower or nap
  • Light housework
  • Evidence-based information about baby care, feeding, sleep, etc
  • Advice of local professionals for lactation, bodywork, mental health, etc.
  • Help with cloth diapering and baby wearing if needed
  • Grocery shopping if needed
  • Sitz baths and loving touch to facilitate oxytocin and bloodflow
  • Belly binding (add-on)

House visits last anywhere from 3 to 6 hours, depending on your needs.


You can book visits out ahead of time, or as needed.


If you are interested in this kind of care, or have more questions, please book a introductory call here.
